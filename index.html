Summary

Hexxco, Inc. is developing a fully integrated hydrogen energy ecosystem — from clean production and refueling infrastructure to vehicle sales. While most companies focus on isolated segments, Hexxco owns the full cycle: solar-powered hydrogen production via electrolysis, on-site storage, retail fueling, and H2 vehicle sales. Our mission is to accelerate the transition from fossil fuels to a sustainable hydrogen future.
